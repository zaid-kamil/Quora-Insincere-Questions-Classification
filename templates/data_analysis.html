Data Analysis


  • Number of sincere questions are much more in number as compared to insincere questions
  • There are some insincere questions having more length as compared to sincere questions
  • Length can be considered a good feature here as the box plots for both the classes are not completly overlapping.There is some variation seen.But it is important to note Length cannot explained the insincerity of the question

WordCloud for Insincere Questions

  • From the WordCloud the words like Muslim,racist,american,Donald Trump,Indian,Pakistan,Gay,Christian and sex seems to be the most important words for declaring a question as an insincere question,Because if the question consists of any of these words than there is high probability of a question being insincere
